Capacitor Printing Services

01

Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors

Aerosol jet and inkjet-printed MLCC structures with interdigitated electrodes in custom geometry. Barium titanate dielectric layers at 5–50µm thickness. Custom footprint and capacitance for RF filtering and power conditioning.

02

Film & Flexible Capacitors

Printed polymer film capacitors on flexible substrates for wearable and conformal electronics. PVDF and P(VDF-TrFE) piezoelectric-capacitive hybrids for energy harvesting and sensing applications.

03

Research & Prototype Batches

Small-batch custom capacitor structures for academic research and pre-commercial evaluation. Material characterization (dielectric constant, loss tangent, breakdown voltage) supplied with every batch.

DIELECTRIC MATERIALS

Capacitor Dielectric Library

The dielectric defines the capacitor. Our material library spans high-k ceramics to flexible polymer films — all printable in custom layer geometries.

  • ● Barium titanate (BaTiO₃) — high-k, k > 1000
  • ● PVDF — flexible, piezoelectric-capacitive
  • ● Alumina (Al₂O₃) — low-loss, RF-grade dielectric
  • ● SrTiO₃ — tunable permittivity for voltage-controlled apps
  • ● Parylene — conformal pinhole-free thin film
  • ● Custom composite dielectric formulations on request
